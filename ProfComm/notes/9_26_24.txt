HATS:
- headings
- access
- typography
- space


readers need information quickly
- writing clear and concise
- documents that are easier to access and understand are more persuasive and user-centered


headings
- provide signposts in table of contents
- help guide readers through doc
- announce forthcoming info
- adequacy: docs should have an ample number of headings (3-4 for our assignment)


access
- finding and understanding info easily
- ensure easy access of important info
- can use graphics


typography
- typeface has persuasive impact and can be changed to improve design
- avoid using more tha 2 types of font
- times new roman for body
- arial or sans serif font such as franklin gothic book for headings


space
- use plenty of space so you dont overwhelm readers
- break up sections
