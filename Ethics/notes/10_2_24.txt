AVs

what are AVs
- a vehicle capable of sensing its environment and moving safely with little or no human input from point A to B
- ADAS - advanced driver assistance systems
  - driving and parking assistance
- CAV: connected autonomous vehicles

why
- the hype with AVs/CAVs: business models around
  - driver safety
  - sustainability
  - democratic mobility, ride and car sharing
  - economic development
  - leaner AI powered supply chain, predictive maintenance, quality control, etc
  - cyber attacks a problem for moving computers

evo of ADAS
- 70s: first ABS introduced
- ADAS requires multiple tech working in conjunction with each other
  - sensing suite acts as eyes of system
